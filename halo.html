Halo 1

As a Halo enthusiast since its launch, I didn't start modding Halo until 2003 beginning with Halo PC trial at the age of 13. In 2004, I had purchased Halo PC and an extra Xbox to mod. By the end of 2004, I was modding Halo 1 Xbox, Halo 1 PC, and creating new levels in Halo CE while lurking Halomods. It wasn't until 2006 I became a member and started sharing content with the community.

Halo 2

As soon as the first modding applications became available, I began modding Halo 2 in 2005. Using the previous knowledge along with a more detailed game, I was able to accomplish more detailed mods. However, I kept finding myself going back to mod Halo 1 instead. My stats from Xbox Live.
